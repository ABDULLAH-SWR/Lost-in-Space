# Lost in Space 🚀
**An entry for the IUT ICT FEST Game Jam — Theme: KickOff**

## 📖 The Story 
After a sudden dark energy storm, you have been abruptly **kicked off** your main fleet! Stranded in zero-gravity, you must use your spacesuit's emergency thrusters to navigate dangerous sectors, dodge obstacles, and slingshot your way to the rescue ship before your oxygen runs out.

## 🎮 Features
* **Zero-G Slingshot Physics:** Click anywhere on the screen and drag to plot your trajectory using dynamic physics lines.
* **Oxygen Management:** You only have 10 seconds of breathable air per launch—speed and precision are critical.
* **Dynamic Obstacles:** Navigate around barriers, space debris, and zero-G puzzles.
* **Custom Object Pooling:** Highly optimized trajectory prediction for smooth performance.

## 🕹️ Controls
* **Aim:** Click anywhere on the screen and drag backward.
* **Launch:** Release the mouse button.
* **Respawn:** Press `R` if you drift off course or run out of oxygen.

* **Engine:** Unity 6
* **Language:** C#
* **Platform:** Windows
